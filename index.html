About Us

Our Work Promise

Sauti Yetu Foundation is an organization that helps people with special needs. We also fight against violence against children and women with special needs. We work together with communities and other organizations to make a difference. We believe that everyone deserves to be treated with respect and dignity, no matter their abilities.

Learn More

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower communities by supporting individuals with special needs, fostering inclusivity, self-reliance, and equal opportunities, and building a society where everyone can thrive independently.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create an inclusive society that values individuals with special needs, fostering understanding, compassion, and equal opportunities for a supportive, caring community where everyone can thrive.

Our Plan

Our plan aims to empower individuals through vocational training, enhance advocacy and community engagement, and expand support services to foster growth, self-reliance, and positive change in communities.

Programs And Services

Educational Support

We provide educational support to empower people with disabilities, children, and women with special needs, fostering inclusion and opportunities.

Health And Wellness

We empower people with disabilities, children, and women with special needs through dedicated health and wellness programs.

Community Engagement

We support people with disabilities, children, and women with special needs through community engagement, and advocating for their rights.

Advocacy And Legal Aid

Our organization empowers people with disabilities, children, and women with special needs by ensuring their rights are protected and fostering equality.

Employment Assistance

Sauti Yetu Foundation empowers people with disabilities, children, and women with special needs through tailored employment assistance programs, fostering inclusion.

Collaboration

Sauti Yetu Foundation builds impactful partnerships to empower people with disabilities, children, and women with special needs through tailored support.

Our Team

Our team brings a wealth of experience and expertise to our work. We are committed to providing high-quality services and support to individuals with special needs, and to advocating for their rights and well-being.
