Program And Services

Our Comprehensive Programs For a Better Tomorrow

At Sauti Yetu Foundation, we are committed to supporting people with special needs in Tanzania through a range of impactful programs. Our Educational Support services aim to provide inclusive learning environments, access to learning materials, and tailored support for students with disabilities. In the area of Health and Wellness, we offer access to essential healthcare services, therapy, and mental health support. Through Community Engagement, we raise awareness and promote inclusion by working with families, schools, and local leaders. Our Advocacy and Legal Aid programs empower individuals to understand their rights and seek justice, while promoting policies that protect and uplift people with special needs. We also provide Employment Assistance by offering skills training, job placement services, and support for workplace inclusion.
These programs are designed to create equal opportunities, enhance independence, and build a more inclusive society for all.
