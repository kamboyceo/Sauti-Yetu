About Sauti Yetu Foundation

Welcome & Let's Build a Better World, Together

Sauti Yetu Foundation Is A Non-Governmental Organization (NGO) That Supports People With Special Needs, Especially Children And Women With Disabilities. We Are Committed To Fighting Violence Against Children And Women With Special Needs, Providing Support To Those Affected, And Ensuring They Are Protected And Have Access To Justice. Our Foundation Also Provides Educational Support To Empower People With Disabilities, Children, And Women With Special Needs, Helping Them Access Learning Opportunities And Fostering Inclusion In Education. We Believe That Everyone, Regardless Of Their Disability, Should Have Equal Opportunities To Learn And Grow.

In Addition, We Offer Health And Wellness Programs To Improve The Physical And Mental Well-Being Of People With Disabilities, Children, And Women With Special Needs. These Programs Help Individuals Lead Healthier, More Independent Lives. We Also Work Through Community Engagement, Raising Awareness About The Needs Of People With Special Needs And Promoting Inclusion In Society. We Advocate For The Rights Of People With Disabilities, Ensuring They Are Treated With Respect And Have Access To The Resources They Need. At Sauti Yetu Foundation, We Are Dedicated To Empowering People With Disabilities And Advocating For Their Rights, Helping Them Live Fulfilling Lives With Dignity And Opportunity.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower communities by supporting individuals with special needs, fostering inclusivity, self-reliance, and equal opportunities, and building a society where everyone can thrive independently.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create an inclusive society that values individuals with special needs, fostering understanding, compassion, and equal opportunities for a supportive, caring community where everyone can thrive.

Our Plan

Our plan aims to empower individuals through vocational training, enhance advocacy and community engagement, and expand support services to foster growth, self-reliance, and positive change in communities.
